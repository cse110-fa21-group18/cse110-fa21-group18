Features

Explore

Tired of cooking and eating the same food everyday? Having trouble thinking about that anniversary dinner surprise? With the explroation feature, we can help solve all your problems. This feature gives you the capabilities of exploring recipes that we have stored in our database from other people. You can search by keyword or by categories and find so many interesting dishes to try and recreate yourself. Don't let your own imagination limit your options, see what the world has to offer and be inspired!.

Create

With the create a recipe feature, we give you the capability of creating recipes of your own choice that can lead to elegant dishes or just experimental trials. This feature also allows you to define recipe titles, list ingredients needed, put them into categories and so much more. With this feature you no longer have to rely on written papers or memorization that can lead to confusion. Once you have created a recipe, it will stored within our database for you to access easily once again in the future.
